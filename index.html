OWEN MURITHI(uncle wa picha)

Hello, I'm Owen Murithii, though most know me as "Uncle wa Picha." I'm a professional photographer, drone pilot, and videographer with a passion for capturing life's most significant moments. Whether it's the joy of a wedding, the solemnity of a burial, or any special event, I'm dedicated to providing you with high-quality, visually compelling memories.

With years of experience in event shooting, I bring a creative eye and technical expertise to every project. I believe in the power of visuals to tell stories, evoke emotions, and create lasting impressions. My approach is not just about taking pictures or videos—it's about crafting a narrative that reflects the essence of your event. Let me help you preserve your memories in the most stunning way possible.
